Hello! I'm Asli.

I enjoy taking complex problems and turning them into simple and beautiful interface designs, I also love the logic and structure of coding and always strive to write elegant and efficient code, whether it be HTML, CSS or JavaScript.

My approach to work and life is directly influenced by Conan O'brien's farewell speech from the Tonight Show: "If you work really hard and you're kind, amazing things will happen." I'm a fan of clean, simple design with high attention to detail and a dash of humour.
